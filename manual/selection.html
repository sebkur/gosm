Selecting

Selections of the map can be made by switching to 'selection'-mode. This is done by toggling the 3rd button in the toolbar, i.e. the 'cross'.

Once the mode is switched, just click on the map for the start of the selection and drag to the end of it.

An existing selection can be adjusted by moving the mouse to the border of it. A slightly darker rectangle will appear which may be dragged to adjust the size of the seleciton
